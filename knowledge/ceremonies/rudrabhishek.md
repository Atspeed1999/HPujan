# Rudrabhishek

## One-liner
A sacred abhishekam of the Shivalinga with Sri Rudram chanting, performed at your home.

## What it is (meaning)
Rudrabhishek is the ritual bathing (abhishekam) of the Shivalinga while the
Sri Rudram — 11 chapters (Namakam & Chamakam) of the Yajurveda — is recited.
It is traditionally performed to dissolve negativity, restore health and
harmony, and draw Lord Shiva's protection over a home and family.

## Why families choose it / benefits
- To clear persistent obstacles and Grah Dosh
- For healing and peace of mind
- Before a major life consolidation
- Simply to invite Shiva's grace into the home

## Who it's for
Households seeking protection, healing, or spiritual advancement; anyone
facing persistent obstacles or wanting Shiva's blessings on a new chapter.

## Ritual steps (how it unfolds)
1. **Sankalpa & Purvanga** — the intention is set; Ganesha and the Navagraha
   are honoured; the space is purified.
2. **Abhishekam with Sri Rudram** — the Shivalinga is bathed with Panchamrit,
   Gangajal and herbs as Namakam & Chamakam are chanted.
3. **Aarti & Prasad** — the ceremony concludes with Aarti, blessings
   (ashirvad), and distribution of prasad to the family.
